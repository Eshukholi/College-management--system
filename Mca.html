Master of Computer Applications(MCA)

To groom and empower students with critical thinking and problem-solving skills to excel in the ever-evolving world of computer applications and contribute to the advancement of technology and society.

Programme Objectives

The main objective of this programme is to provide the students in depth knowledge about the computer science. It aims to prepare students for careers in software industry such as system analysts, system designer, programmers etc.

Admission Eligibility Criteria

For admission to first year of MCA, a candidate must have passed the bachelor level degree course of three years from any recognized University or its equivalent, securing minimum 50% (45% for SC/ST) marks in aggregate. Candidate must have passed mathematics at 10+2 level or graduation level. For getting admission, a candidate has to qualify entrance exams such as UPSEE, MAT etc. with a good score. These entrance tests contain the objective questions on verbal ability, quantitative ability, reading comprehension and data interpretation etc..
