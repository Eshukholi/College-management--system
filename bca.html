Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA)

The BCA programme is recognized by the Government of Uttar Pradesh and affiliated to CCS University, Meerut. It is a three-year full-time degree programme divided into six semesters including project work.

Programme Objectives

To develop skilled computer professionals with practical and theoretical knowledge in computing, programming, and applications.

Eligibility Criteria

The students who want to take admission in BCA, should have passed 10+2 level examination or any other equivalent to CBSE or ICSE or any other recognized board.

Syllabus

  • Semester 1: Fundamentals of Computers, Programming Basics, Mathematics I
  • Semester 2: Programming in C, Computer Organization, Mathematics II
  • Semester 3: Data Structures, Database Management Systems, Operating Systems
  • Semester 4: Object Oriented Programming (C++), Web Technologies, Software Engineering
  • Semester 5: Java Programming, Networking, Elective I
  • Semester 6: Project Work, Mobile App Development, Elective II
