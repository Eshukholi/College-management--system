Department of Engineering

Vision

To foster innovation, technical excellence, and leadership in engineering education and research, producing competent engineers for the future.

Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech)

The Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) program equips students with practical knowledge and technical expertise to excel in the engineering field.

Programme Objectives

  • To provide strong foundational knowledge in engineering and technology.
  • To develop problem-solving and analytical skills through real-world applications.
  • To promote innovation, teamwork, and ethical values.

Future Scope

B.Tech graduates have vast opportunities in sectors such as IT, manufacturing, research, public services, and entrepreneurship. They can also pursue M.Tech or MBA for advanced studies.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum 50% marks in 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
  • Qualifying score in entrance exams like JEE Main or state-level tests.
  • Direct admission for diploma holders through lateral entry.
