CHAIRPERSON GLOBAL INSTITUTE OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

Dr. Vinod Singh

B.Tech | MBA | Ph.D

Dr. Vinod Singh is the Chairman of Global Institute of Information Technology and is one of the founder trustee of the Global Education Trust. He obtained his B.Tech from University of Pune and MBA from Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University, Delhi with distinction.

He started his career as a lecturer in an engineering college and moved to the corporate profile for a few years. After his stint in the corporate world, he started his business journey with the establishment of Bharat Computers, Jind Institute of Engineering and Technology (founder trustee), Ganga Agro Foods and Global Institute of Information Technology.

A reflection of his commitment towards research was him being awarded a Ph.D. in Finance from Gautam Buddh Technical University (GBTU). His research work has been and is widely published in various reputed national and international journals.
