Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech)

To groom and empower students with critical thinking and problem-solving skills to excel in the ever-evolving world of technology and contribute to the advancement of society.

Programme Objectives

The main objective of this programme is to provide students with in-depth knowledge of computer science and engineering. It aims to prepare students for careers in software development, system analysis, network management, and research.

Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must have passed 10+2 (or equivalent) with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as compulsory subjects from a recognized board. A minimum aggregate of 60% marks is generally required.

Admission is usually based on entrance exams like JEE Main / State CET or the institute’s own entrance test followed by counseling or interview rounds.

Future Scope

B.Tech graduates have a vast range of career opportunities across industries. They can pursue roles in:

  • Software Development and IT Services
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Cybersecurity and Cloud Computing
  • Research and Development
  • Data Science and Analytics
  • Entrepreneurship or Higher Studies (M.Tech, MBA, MS)
