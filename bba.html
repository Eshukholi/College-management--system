Department of Business Administration

Vision

To shape future business leaders with strong ethical values, analytical thinking, and global perspectives to contribute to organizational excellence and economic development.

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

The BBA program provides students with a solid foundation in management principles, business operations, finance, marketing, and entrepreneurship. It prepares them for diverse roles in corporate, government, and entrepreneurial sectors.

Programme Objectives

  • To equip students with comprehensive business knowledge and management skills.
  • To develop leadership qualities and decision-making abilities.
  • To encourage entrepreneurial thinking and innovation.
  • To prepare students for higher studies such as MBA or for immediate entry into professional careers.

Future Scope

BBA graduates can pursue careers in banking, marketing, finance, HR, and administration. Many go on to complete their MBA or start their own businesses. With globalization, BBA graduates are in demand across multinational companies and startups alike.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Completion of 10+2 (any stream) from a recognized board.
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks (45% for reserved categories).
  • Admissions based on merit or entrance tests as per college norms.
