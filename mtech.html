Department of Engineering

Vision

To advance engineering research and innovation by producing highly skilled technocrats equipped with deep technical knowledge and leadership abilities to drive industrial and academic excellence.

Master of Technology (M.Tech)

The M.Tech program is a postgraduate degree that builds upon the technical foundations acquired during undergraduate studies. It focuses on specialized domains such as Computer Science, Mechanical, Electrical, Civil, and Electronics Engineering, promoting advanced learning and research.

Programme Objectives

  • To equip students with advanced technical skills and research-oriented learning.
  • To foster innovation and problem-solving through projects and industrial collaboration.
  • To prepare students for careers in academia, research, and industry leadership roles.
  • To encourage lifelong learning and continuous professional development.

Future Scope

Graduates of the M.Tech program can pursue careers in research, design, academia, and technology development. They are highly sought after by industries in domains such as AI, robotics, manufacturing, data analytics, and sustainable technology. Many also go on to pursue doctoral studies (Ph.D.).

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Engineering/Technology (B.E./B.Tech) with at least 55% aggregate marks.
  • Valid GATE score or equivalent entrance examination qualification.
  • Final-year students awaiting results can also apply provisionally.
